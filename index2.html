VIDEOS

Animations and Video works created in Adobe Premiere

I began to explore video art primarily through my first few years of undergrad, around the same time I had found enjoyment in sculpture, which eventually lead to 3D art. I found video to be a unique platform to express my creativity and design when approaching a certain tone for an audience. I now enjoy both outlets to showcase the environments and assets I create.

As a digital artist, I am always seeking unique and challenging ways to reimagine perspective within my work. The cameras both in real life and in these softwares have endless amounts of utilization for expressing an organic experience for my audience.
