Renders

Left & Right

After building my own computer, I immedietly began diving into the world of 3D modeling. I felt a distantly familiar excitement, to when I had hours in my day as a child to create whatever scene I wanted with the many LEGO sets I had been gifted. My renders are a mix of personal projects and client work. While I do thoroughly enjoy the process of creating individual assets, my real passion I believe is within crafting environments and captivating scenes.

Much of my artistic inspiration comes from the many places I have been to in my life so far. From California to Mexico to Beirut and Bali, I am beyond grateful to have had the ability to explore such a vast array of design, creativity, and adaptation across so many cultures. I love exploring new styles of architecture within my scenes depending on what kind of mood I am trying to express, usually somewhere along the creation process I just start doing what I think works and sometimes it ends up how I want it.
